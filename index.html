THE BAND

We Love Hard Rock Music

Rock4PEACE is an international band with five professional members, it was founded in 2012 by Bresciani Renato (Italian), he has 40 years experience playing hard rock music. The band consist of two Lead guitar players, a Basist, one Drummer and Vocalist with Ten crew members including the sound engineers, light engineers and others. The idea of bringing hard rock genre into Nigeria for the citizens to have a taste of the music played in the western world. Hard Rock music is very interesting with energetic live performance. Our rehearsing studio is in Lekki phase1. Rock4Peace band is open for an open ground concert shows for new brand publicity and big outdoor events. Our target is to make wonderful performance everywhere we go
